    Combat Robotics in Three Days #

    Welcome to Combat Robotics in Three Days (CRi3D). Our mission is to spread the fun of combat robotics, with a focus on affordable, approachable ways to get started. #

    Origins #

    CRi3d is inspired by the ‘Robot in 3 Days (Ri3D)’ challenge, which is done every year as part of the kickoff week for the First Robotics Competition season. Using that as a model, we set out to design, manufacture, wire, and assemble a small combat robot within 3 days. We then immediately take the robots to compete, so there’s no cheating!

    We have lots of content planned! But while we get our ducks in a row on that, here’s a peek as to what’s to come…

    CRi3D Debut #

    At DragonCon 2022, we hosted 3 sessions as part of the Robotics and Maker track as our inaugural session. We succeeded in building 2 antweight robots on-site and competing in Robot Battles, which is hosted at the convention every year.
